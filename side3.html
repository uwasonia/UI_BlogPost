Eam in docendi tibique.


Was it love at first sight
Or the night became short...?

She saw him at the dance floor
The guy with a shinny blue suit
Dancing like he owns the muse
And very passionate
Made her eyes only see him.

Dreams were like wishes
Atleast one touch was enough
Until her diamond eyes attracted him
And he grabs her to join his moves.

Everything was too fast
But the music went too slow
They danced silently
And their heart falls suddenly
That's when l like you came up.

She was scared and pleased
Selfmade question asked
Their eyes never closed
His name was Theodore Bonafasio
Very handsome than his name.

His phone rang
It was a call from army, he was a going back.

Saying goodbye was hard
And smiles of goodbye was sad
She wished he can stay
( just a little longer )
But the country needed him than her.

A nice genthle voice of him
keep spinning her head down
She can't stop think each of that moment
He would have said I love you and I'll come back instead.


        - Sonia -
